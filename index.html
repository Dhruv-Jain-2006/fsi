White Marbles

Representing purity and peace, white marble is timeless and hence always in trend. Works well with neutral tones (creams, blacks or greys) while when combined with red or green helps to soften ambiences.

Beige Marbles

Beige marble showcases the areas excellently. Its light colour look lends a warm and welcoming feeling to space. They add an ultimate glow and lustre that displays a sparkling vibrancy, regardless of its application.

Onyx

Richly patterned and translucent, the exotic onyx as a decorative accent, infuses exquisite luxe into a home’s decor. This stone is unique because of its rich layers and veining that are naturally formed due to the deposit of quartz and moganite over decades.

Travertine

A natural stone derived from the natural springs, travertine is one of the oldest building materials. It is an excellent choice for people who love warm colours and exclusive textures. Ideal for contemporary spaces, and can be matched with other stones with ease and comfort.
